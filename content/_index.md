# About Me
Since October 2025 I am a PhD student at the [University of Bonn](https://www.math.uni-bonn.de/ag/topo/) and the [Max Planck Institute for Mathematics](https://www.mpim-bonn.mpg.de/), supervised by [Markus Hausmann](https://www.math.uni-bonn.de/people/hausmann/).   
I am particularly interested in equivariant and chromatic homotopy theory.
