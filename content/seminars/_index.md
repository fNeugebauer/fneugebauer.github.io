## Motivic Homotopy Theory (winter 25/26)

In the winter semester 2025/26, [Lucas Piessevaux](https://lucas-piessevaux.github.io), [Qi Zhu](https://qizhumath.wixsite.com/math), and I organized a seminar on motivic homotopy theory. We started from the basics and worked our way up to the Hopkins–Morel–Hoyois theorem, as well as non-$A^1$-invariant motivic homotopy theory.  

You can find the [syllabus](/files/motivic_syllabus_08_12_2025.pdf) here. I typed up [notes](/files/setup_of_motivic_homotopy_theory.pdf) for the first talk and Qi [live-texed notes](https://qizhumath.wixsite.com/math/teaching) during most of the seminar talks.
