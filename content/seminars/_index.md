### Motivic Homotopy Theory

In the winter semester 2025/26, [Lucas Piessevaux](https://lucas-piessevaux.github.io), [Qi Zhu](https://qizhumath.wixsite.com/math), and I are organizing a seminar on motivic homotopy theory. We’ll start from the basics and work our way up to the Hopkins–Morel–Hoyois theorem, as well as non-$A^1$-invariant motivic homotopy theory.  

You can find the [syllabus](/files/motivic_syllabus_15_08_2025.pdf) and the [participation form](https://forms.gle/UukfAK6XULW9KMAu8) here.
